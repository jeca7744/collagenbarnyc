Who we are

Collagen Bar is a premier skin clinic and medical spa specializing in collagen restoration for all ages. With a flagship location in Gramercy Park and satellite offices in Midtown, the Upper East Side, and Harrison, NY, we provide cutting-edge, science-backed treatments.

Led by a team with over 20 years of combined expertise, Collagen Bar offers innovative services including the Neo Collagenizer with microcurrent, Forma Facials, Morpheus8®, Skinpen RF microneedling, IPL, LaseMD Thulium Laser, Laser Genesis, Excel V+, and the Alma TED hair growth device.

At Collagen Bar, clients of all ages experience tighter, smoother, and more youthful skin and hair, thanks to our team’s decades of expertise and commitment to delivering long-lasting results.
